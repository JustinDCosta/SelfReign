# Privacy Policy — SelfReign

**Last updated: June 24, 2026**

SelfReign ("the app") is developed by Aldren Studios ("we", "us"). This policy
explains how the app handles your information. In short: **we don't collect any of
it.**

## The short version
- SelfReign collects **no personal data**.
- It has **no user accounts**, **no ads**, and **no analytics or tracking**.
- The app makes **no network requests** of its own and has **no internet permission**.
- Everything you enter stays **on your device**, stored **encrypted**.

## Information we collect
**None.** We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.
We have no servers and never receive your data.

## Data stored on your device
All of your activity — your streak start time, relapse history (and any optional
notes or trigger tags you add), milestones, money-saved settings, and preferences —
is stored **locally on your device only**. Sensitive recovery data is encrypted at
rest using the Android Keystore (AES-256). None of it leaves your device.

If you set an app-lock PIN, it is stored only as a salted hash on your device and is
never included in any backup.

## Backups you create
The app lets you manually export your data to a JSON file that **you** choose where
to save. That file is under your control; we never see it. Importing a file only
reads data you select.

## Permissions
- **Notifications** (Android 13+): used only to show the optional daily encouragement
  reminder, if you enable it. You can decline or revoke this at any time.
- **Vibrate**: used only for optional haptic feedback, which is **off by default**.

## Links that leave the app
If you choose to tap the optional "Buy me a coffee" support link in Settings, your
device's browser opens our Ko-fi page (`https://ko-fi.com/aldrenstudios`). Anything
you do there is governed by **Ko-fi's** privacy policy, not this one. The app itself
sends no data; it simply hands the link to your browser.

## Children
SelfReign is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any
information from anyone.

## Deleting your data
Because everything is stored locally, **uninstalling the app permanently deletes all
of your data** from your device. Any backup files you exported remain wherever you
saved them until you delete them.

## Changes to this policy
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "last
updated" date.
